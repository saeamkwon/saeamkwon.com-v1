About Quad

Quad is an edtech startup that aims to help college students have a better experience. Quad helps helps students keep track of their assignments so that they can worry less and enjoy their college experience.

An Approachable Style

Quad's initial style guide had the app go for a sleek and sophisticated deisgn. But since their target audience is college students, the direction was taken into a more approachable design with a light base and colorful illustrations.
